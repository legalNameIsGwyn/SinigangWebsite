Organizations to Mitigate Global Warming

Centre for Sustainability Philippines Inc.

“Our Work comes from Communities, is for Communities, and goes to Communities.”

The Centre for Sustainability PH, Inc. is a women-led, youth, environmental non-profit organization which focuses on conserving land and protecting the remaining forests of the country through officially declaring legal protected areas.

They do reforestation, regenerating forests through preserving the native tree species in collaboration with indigenous & local communities. Conserve biodiversity areas through leading scientific research and political lobbying. Moreover, educate and let indigenous & local communities conduct independent biodiversity checks in the forests they live in and around.

YACAP (Youth Advocates for Climate Actions Philippines)

“Fighting for our present and future”

YACAP is the counterpart of Fridays for Future in the Philippines. This organization has the youth organizations, individuals, and student councils collaboration around the country that advocate for urgent mitigation of climate change. They envision that the youth, along with the most marginalized segments of society, lead the ongoing worldwide fight against climate change to ensure the survival of our planet.

They demand justice for the climate from corporations and governments who have contributed the most to the degradation of the environment. Hence, recognizing the urgency of climate action based on science and the need for informing people about it. To prevent further global degradation, there must be a system-wide scale of change and action to transition to a pro-environment system.

Adaptation solutions vary from across the globe however, understanding local risks and developing plans can help manage climate change.

Healthcare for Clean Air Alliance

Formed in 2018 and composed of health and environmental advocates, doctors, students and government agencies, the alliance is using a series of environmental campaigns to frame air pollution not just as an environmental problem but also as a health menace.

This organization aims to highlight the health impacts of air pollution by pushing for the strict implementation of laws against air pollutants and helping improve the country’s air-quality monitoring standards.

Philippine Business for the Environment

This Non-Government Organization built to help aspiring businessmen that plan to open a business in the Philippines and look after the environment. The Philippines offers business opportunities, and the organization guides these businesses to start or flourish through a sustainable approach. Importantly, They believe that businesses also need to have a corporate responsibility to conserve the environment by applying different sustainable approaches.

Philippine Business for the Environment supports business from its start through its successive operation. To enable businesses in the country to deliver a positive impact is among the goals of the organization. Also, built to assist environmental concerns and challenges in a business within the country.
