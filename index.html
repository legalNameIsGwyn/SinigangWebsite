Future Consequences

The Philippines is one of the most affected countries because of Climate Change. According to a report made in 2017, it is the third most vulnerable country due to the impacts of climate change. This is noticeable in the temperature changes and precipitation patterns. The magnitude of the disasters that are taking place in the country is also an effect of Climate Change. Apart from all these, more impacts include losses in GDP, rainfall pattern changes, droughts, threats to biodiversity and food, sea level rise, public health risks, and endangerment of groups such as women and indigenous people. If all these issues are not addressed and resolved, the future of our country and generations will also be affected to a greater extent than what we are experiencing today.
